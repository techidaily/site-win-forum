### Method 4: Disable 3rd-Party Explorer Extensions

You will ask ‚Äì but what are 3rd-party Explorer extensions?

 They are quality of life improvements ‚Äì for example, if you install 7-Zip you can directly right-click a file and compress it, instead of launching the software.

 If a 3rd-party extension becomes unstable it could crash the explorer.exe process.

 To fix this issue you will need to disable them with a third-party app called ShellExView.
